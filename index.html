About us

Incorporated in September 2021, Swift-Lane Cab Services Limited began its full commercial operations in December 2021. We are a fully registered transport company dedicated to providing luxury car transport services with unparalleled comfort and convenience.

Our Uniqueness

At Swift-Lane, we pride ourselves on offering a premium travel experience for our esteemed passengers. Our services include:


Free Internet Service

 Stay connected throughout your journey with complimentary Wi-Fi.

Fully Air-Conditioned Vehicles

 Enjoy a comfortable and refreshing ride in our top-of-the-line vehicles.

Complementary Snacks

 Indulge in delicious snacks during your trip to enhance your travel experience.

Specializing in professional and corporate transport services, we operate routes between Abuja and Gombe, with convenient stops in Keffi, Akwanga, Jos, and Bauchi. As the first private transport company to offer luxury services in the entire North-Eastern part of Nigeria, we also take pride in being the first to introduce an online ticketing system, making it easier than ever to book your travel.

Our services are specifically designed for high-profile travelers who prefer a more luxurious alternative to airline services.

Our Goal
At Swift-Lane, our primary goal is CUSTOMER SATISFACTION. We strive to exceed your expectations and ensure that every journey with us is a remarkable experience.
